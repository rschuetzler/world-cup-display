128 × 64 RGB Matrix · World Cup 2026

The same high-density panel, repurposed for the summer tournament — pared down to the two boards that earn their place. Now & Next answers the only two questions that matter from across the room: what's the score right now, and when does the next one kick off. Live match is the single-game hero — big national flags, big score, the running minute and a pitch-progress bar. Both render at the true 128×64 grid as round LEDs with a touch of bloom, with flags drawn as LED pixels and live minutes / countdowns ticking against the wall clock.

128×64 logical px 2 displays flag + 3-letter code identity live minutes · kickoff countdowns flag-color tint
Click either panel to inspect at scale (← / → to step, Esc to close). Colors: red = live, gold = minute / kickoff time, green = pitch progress, flag accents tint the team codes. Open Tweaks to scrub the live minute, set the score, swap codes ↔ country names, or toggle the flags.
